What tasks did workers do?

You will see some information about the workers to help you make the hiring decision.

These workers have already participated in two rounds of tasks.

These workers were required to answer 5 easy SAT math questions in Round 1 and another 5 hard SAT math questions in Round 2. Workers were incentivized to answer as many correct questions as possible. In other words, they were paid more if they answered more questions correctly. They had five minutes available for each round of the task, and were not allowed to use a calculator.

You may or may not receive information on how well the workers performed in Round 1, your job is to predict who performed the best in Round 2. And your payment will be determined by how the workers you choose actually performed in Round 2.
